PAKISTAN'S BUSINESS CLIMATE

An Attractive Destination for Investors

"Recently, Pakistan has significantly improved in the 'Doing Business Ranking' issued by the World Bank. In 2020 alone, Pakistan moved up 28 positions to rank 108th, thanks to progressive regulatory reforms introduced by SECP. This makes Pakistan an attractive destination for investors."

The Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP) has implemented numerous reforms to simplify business registration, improve investor protection, and streamline regulatory processes. These changes have created a more favorable environment for both domestic and foreign investment.
