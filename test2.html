Pakistan's Business Climate

Discover why Pakistan is becoming an increasingly attractive destination for investors and businesses.

Pakistan has been making significant strides in creating a more favorable business environment for both domestic and international investors. The government has implemented numerous reforms to simplify procedures and improve the ease of doing business.

"Recently, Pakistan has significantly improved in the 'Doing Business Ranking' issued by the World Bank. In 2020 alone, Pakistan moved up 28 positions to rank 108th, thanks to progressive regulatory reforms introduced by SECP. This makes Pakistan an attractive destination for investors."

With ongoing reforms and improving economic indicators, now is an excellent time to explore business opportunities in Pakistan. Our team at Infinitax can help you navigate the regulatory landscape and take advantage of these positive developments.
