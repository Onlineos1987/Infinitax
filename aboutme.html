About Me Syed Jamal Ahmed Zaidi

Syed Jamal Ahmed Zaidi is the Managing Partner/ Chief Executive of the Firm. He holds two fellowships, first, as a Chartered Management Accountant from the Institute of Cost & Management Accountants of Pakistan (ICMAP) and, second, as a Chartered Certified Accountant from the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA), UK. He also holds an LLB from University of Punjab, Pakistan.

During his 22-years of experience with the industry and the regulators being State Bank of Pakistan (SBP) and the Securities & Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP), Mr. Zaidi has dealt a horizontal spread of projects including product development, compliance, enforcement and conventional financial-cum-accounting portfolios. At SECP, being his latest stint before joining the practice, he has served in key operational departments being Non-Banking Finance Companies/ Specialized Companies Department and Company Law Division.

At Infinitax, he is assisted by high-caliber professionals including:

  • E-1: Technocrats-cum-tax advisory experts who have served in FBR as in top positions.
  • E-2: Audit & Assurance, Corporate Reporting and public financial management experts who have delivered projects, both locally and overseas.
  • E-3: Litigation experts including barristers and Supreme Court practitioners with expertise in corporate/ business cases.
  • E-4: Financial modelling/ transaction advisory experts.
