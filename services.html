Including public limited companies, private limited companies, not-for-profit associations/NGOs, limited liability partnerships, formation of trusts, societies, partnership firms/AOPs, and sole proprietorships.

Business start-up advisory and sustainability counselling, including SECP, SBP, PEC, OGRA, PTA, NEPRA, and license renewals. This also includes licensed businesses such as NBFCs, Banks, DFIs, Brokerage houses, Security companies, and exchange companies.

We handle the filing of all returns (Form A, 29, 45, accounts, petitions for change of objects/province, application for change of name/status, etc.), maintenance of registers and records of clients, and act as their back office under mandatory company law provisions.
